About Keystone Water Initiative

Who We Are

Keystone Water Initiative is an independent environmental engineering effort focused on Pennsylvania water quality, watershed restoration, legacy pollution, and public education.

Our mission is to investigate overlooked environmental problems, communicate findings clearly to the public, and promote science-based restoration solutions for communities and ecosystems.

We focus especially on acid mine drainage, watershed degradation, infrastructure impacts, ecological health, and environmental accountability across Pennsylvania and the Appalachian region.
